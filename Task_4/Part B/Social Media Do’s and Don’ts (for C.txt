							Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (for College Students)

Using social media wisely is an important part of being digitally responsible. Here are some simple guidelines to follow:

Do’s--

1.Think before you post—ask yourself if it’s appropriate and reflects you well.
2.Keep your profile private and secure by adjusting privacy settings.
3.Share only verified information to avoid spreading rumors or fake news.
4.Use social media in a positive and professional way, as it can influence your future opportunities.
5.Be respectful and kind when interacting with others online.

Don’ts--

1.Don’t share personal or sensitive details like your address, phone number, or financial information.
2.Avoid posting anything offensive or harmful, even as a joke.
3.Don’t accept friend or follow requests from strangers without checking their authenticity.
4.Avoid oversharing your personal life, as it can be misused.
5.Don’t get involved in online fights or arguments, as they can damage your image.

Following these simple habits can help you stay safe and maintain a positive digital presence.
